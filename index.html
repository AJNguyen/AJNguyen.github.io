Personality Profile

Myer-Briggs Personality Test
Result: The Advocate (INFJ-T)

The results of the Myer-Briggs personality test don’t really mean much to me as they are just vague statements that can apply to anyone given the correct situation or certain situation. There is also no certainty that results are accurate due to people changing their answers depending on their current mood or deciding on an ideal answer. The results won’t influence my behaviour on any team as I will continue doing what I do in group assignment, which is work together to achieve the most optimal result. Using the test to form teams won’t really achieve much as it will depend entirely on how much people are willing to participate on the work.

Online Learning Style Test
Result: Visual Learner

Just like the Myer-Briggs test, the learning style test result don’t really mean much to me as I too see them as vague statements that can be applied to any users given the correct circumstances. However, the results of the test can be interesting when understanding how to help team members understand different type of problems. The result will allow me to see the best approach to help someone to understand how to complete a task, via visual, sounding or mixture of both..

DISC Personality Test

This isn’t going to influence on how I act with my team, I’m going to put in the work and I’m going to do what I can to complete a task.
